Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This page explains, in plain language, what information we collect and what we do with it.

What We Collect

This site and the EWS-Lite portal demo only collect basic metadata — things like which pages were visited, browser type, and approximate usage statistics. We do not collect or store sensitive personal data through this site.

What We Don't Collect

No Credential Storage

We do not store AWS credentials, passwords, or account secrets through this website or the demo portal. The "Sign In" and dashboard areas you see here are UI demos with mock data — no real credentials are ever requested or saved.

No Resale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share any information collected through this site with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Contact Forms

If you submit the contact or onboarding form, we use the details you provide (name, email, company, message) only to respond to your inquiry. We don't add you to a mailing list or share that information outside our team.

Cookies & Tracking

This site does not use advertising trackers or sell browsing data. Any basic analytics used are solely to understand which pages are useful to visitors.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated directly to active clients.
